# LabEx Pro: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## How does LabEx differentiate from other learning platforms?

LabEx is all about labs - no videos. We truly believe that you learn the best by doing. With our AI-powered assistant, Labby, by your side 24/7, every challenge faced becomes a learning opportunity.

## How do I start my learning journey with LabEx Pro?

LabEx Pro unlocks a universe of over 6000 labs and 30 Skill Trees tailored for you. If you're a novice, kick off your journey by selecting a Skill Tree and beginning with our recommended starter courses. For those already grounded in the basics, filter by specific skills and handpick labs that suit your learning appetite. Dive deep, learn authentically.

## Are there discounts for students or group subscriptions?  

Yes, we offer special discounts for students and group subscriptions. For more details, please get in touch with us via email at `info@labex.io`.

## What if my saved virtual machines exceed the allowed limit?  

With LabEx Pro, you can preserve up to 2 virtual machines. If your virtual machines surpass the LabEx Pro limits, you would need to delete some existing ones to free up space.

## Why aren't Alibaba Cloud labs included in LabEx Pro?  

Due to cost considerations, Alibaba Cloud isn't incorporated presently. To conduct labs on Alibaba Cloud, you'll need to purchase credits separately. Be aware, this might limit the number of your lab attempts.

## Can I cancel my subscription?  

Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime. When you cancel, you'll continue to have access to all LabEx Pro features until your current billing cycle ends.

To cancel, simply go to your profile, select the 'Subscriptions' tab, and there you'll find the Cancel option.

## Can I switch from a monthly to an annual subscription?  

Yes, you can make the switch anytime. When you choose to move to the annual subscription, your monthly subscription will terminate at the end of the current month, paving the way for the annual cycle.
